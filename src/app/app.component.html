FAQs

When and how much is the mint?

Mint date for the 6 Starting biomes will be on 2nd quarter of year 2022 for 100 ADA each and will only have 600 supply. Expansion biomes will be on 3rd quarter and Land plots 4th quarter of 2022.

Is the game already built and playable?

Not yet. Game development will start on Q3 2022 and will be built on top of a minecraft server for POC. Development of stand alone game will start on 1st quarter of 2023. More details will be release on our whitepaper 4th quarter of 2022.

How will you be able to provide utility and value to the holders if the game is not yet playable?

we will provide utility and value by giving holders access to exclusive discord channels (alpha channel,DAO, WL opportunities to other projects etc) and tools that will help them in their crypto and nft venture.

How will the mint proceeds be used?

75% of the mint proceeds will be used fo game development. 10% will be used for marketing and social developments. The other 15% will be used for the upcoming $SPACE token liquidity pool.
